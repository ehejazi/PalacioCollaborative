Jodie Kyle
Senior Mechanical Cost Manager

Jodie Kyle is a Senior Mechanical Cost Manager with over twenty-five years of cost estimating and consulting experience. Jodie prepares quantity take-off, labor, material and equipment pricing for HVAC, fire protection and plumbing elements. Over the past twenty-five years, Jodie has prepared cost estimates for more than 3,000 new build and renovation projects including STEM Higher Education facilities, Healthcare, Government, and Municipal building projects. Jodie Kyle has worked in MCACES, MII, CSI and most other estimating formats.
