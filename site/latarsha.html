A Few Words

W. David Stoutamire has over thirty-five years of experience in the design and construction industry. Throughout his career, David has been responsible for selling and managing corporate, government, and institutional Project/Program Management engagements for clients both nationally and internationally. David’s market sector experience includes Higher Education, Commercial Office, Industrial Distribution, Data Centers, Manufacturing, and Healthcare. Before partnering with Palacio Collaborative, David formerly was Operations Director of the Mace Group, one of the world’s largest international Project and Program Management firms. David was previously Principal/Owner of Sempai Services, a boutique Project Management services firm. He began his career as an Officer in the US Army Corps of Engineers, the world’s largest Construction Program Management organization.

Mr. Stoutamire is a distinguished graduate of the United States Military Academy at West Point, New York. During military service, he earned advanced degrees in engineering from the University of Florida (Civil Engineering and Construction Management). After moving to Atlanta, he earned his MBA from Emory University.
